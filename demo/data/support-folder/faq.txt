How do I reset my password? Use the account recovery link we email you and choose a new passphrase.
Can I get a refund? Yes — money back is available within thirty days of a charge, returned to the original card.
Why was I charged twice? A pre-authorization hold can look like a second charge; it drops off in a few business days.
How do I change my plan? Upgrade or downgrade from Settings, Billing; changes are prorated for the current cycle.
