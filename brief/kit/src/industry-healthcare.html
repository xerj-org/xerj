XERJ.AI02 · HEALTHCARE AI PROBLEM
02·WHY THIS MATTERS NOW

AI IN CARE
MEETS THE
PHI BOUNDARY.

Every health system has an AI strategy: ambient documentation, clinical decision support, patient-portal copilots, claims automation, research RAG. Every one of them stalls at the data layer — PHI cannot leave the perimeter, and the retrieval stacks that commercial AI vendors ship require it to.

PHI SOVEREIGNTY
HIPAA · GDPR · provincial / national health-data laws. Patient data cannot be embedded in a vendor's cloud retrieval service. The retrieval layer must run inside the provider's perimeter.
CLINICAL LATENCY
Ambient scribes, CDS alerts, MAR reconciliation — retrieval latencies above ~200 ms disrupt the clinical workflow. Most vendor RAG stacks exceed that once orchestration is added.
FRAGMENTED STACKS
EHR analytics on one store, embeddings on another, audit logs on a third. A PHI-access audit crosses three systems with three RBAC models. Compliance becomes adversarial.
RESEARCH SILOS
Genomics, imaging, publication corpus, clinical trials · each in a separate retrieval tool. Researcher RAG productivity stalls on retrieval-stack migration, not on model capability.
